Privacy Policy for Beat Box Music Player
Effective Date: 06/05/2025

Beat Box Music Player ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles user data, device information, and third-party services in compliance with Google Play's User Data Policy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). Specifically:

No account registration is required

We do not request your name, phone number, address, or other personal identifiers

We do not collect usage behavior or analytics tied to your identity

2. Optional Google Email for YouTube Data

Users may optionally provide a Google email to sync personal YouTube playlists or data. This is handled as follows:

The email is only used locally on your device to load YouTube-related content

We do not store or transmit this data to our servers

This information is automatically deleted when the app is uninstalled

3. Device Data and Third-Party SDKs

We use third-party SDKs to monetize the app (e.g., for ads). These SDKs may access non-personal device information, such as:

Device type and model

Operating system version

Country or region (for ad targeting)

These SDKs do not access or share personal data, and are selected based on their compliance with privacy standards.


4. Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us.

5. Data Retention and Deletion
No user data is stored on our servers

Any data (such as synced YouTube playlists) exists only on your device

Uninstalling the app will completely remove all locally stored data

6. Your Privacy Choices

You can:

Decline to enter a Google email and still use the app fully

Uninstall the app at any time to delete all data
